Trust in AI Systems in Physiotherapy

DO YOU TRUST AI IN YOUR CLINICAL PRACTICE?

Welcome

We are investigating how physiotherapy professionals perceive and trust artificial intelligence tools.

What do you have to do?

We will present you with 5 tasks. In each, you will see 3 scenarios (systems) with different characteristics, like a combo plate.

You have to choose the one that offers you the most confidence. It will be highlighted in blue and allow you to proceed.

There are no right or wrong answers, we are interested in your genuine preferences.

Estimated time: 6-8 minutes.

Data protection and confidentiality:

Confidenciality: This survey is completely ANONYMOUS. No personally identifiable data is collected.

Purpose: The collected data will be used exclusively for scientific research purposes within the framework of the project "CONFIIA: Trust and New Forms of Integration of Artificial Intelligence" (PID2024-156166OA-I00), funded by the Ministry of Science, Innovation and Universities.

Voluntary nature: Your participation is completely voluntary. You may leave the survey at any time without consequences.
